Welcome to Exercise Journal


This app will allow you to log all of your daily exercises. This way you can track your progress in an organized and efficient manner to reach your fitness goals.

By using this application, you will be able to monitor the following:

  • Date
  • Exercise/Cardio
  • Weight/Time
  • Sets
  • Repetitions
